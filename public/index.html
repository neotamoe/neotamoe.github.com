Sci123 Quiz App

Solo project created during a two week period during my time at Prime Digital Academy. The quiz app is designed to complement a general education science course at Augsburg College (Sci123: Science of Food and Cooking). This full stack web application includes a mongoDB with over 1000 quiz questions. Questions are sorted by chapter as well as assigned up to 5 tags. Students sign in to quiz themself on course content or submit a new question for inclusion in the database. Questions can be selected by chapter or by tag/keyword. Application administrator is notified by email when a student flags a question for review or submits a question for inclusion in the databse. The administrator can edit, save or delete flagged or newly-submitted questions from the database.
